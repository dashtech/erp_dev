Odoo 10 : Car Repair Management

This module is fantastic solution for repair industry , which include any sort of machine repair , here car repair specifically focus on automobile and fleet repair, which cover all aspect of repairing process it is good solution for repair industry which include , car workshop, automobile spartshop, garage, for any Car that needs repairing , generated diagnosis and work order , system will create Sale order and Invoice for the whole process done on the Car



How to use



  • Car Repair:This is the first step where all the information related to Car as well as Customer will be filled.Once saved, system will lead you to next step,creating Car Diagnosis.




  • Car Repair Report: Reports for Car Repair receipt and Car Repair Lable can be generated.






  • Car Diagnosis: In this step, Technician will be assigned ,who will diagnose the Car in future. If any part needs replacement or repairing that will be added,too.








  • Car Diagnosis and Sale: Once Diagnosis create after, you will have to create quotation. After confirming it, Work order related to this sale order will be generated.






  • Car Diagnosis Report: These reports will have all the data about diagnosis of the Car like, diagnosis results, parts details, etc. 1) Car Diagnostic Request 2) Car Diagnostic Result




  • Car Workorders: Now, worker will be able to start, pause , put in pending , cancel and finish the work order as per progress of the work on the Car. When work order is done, according to the hours spent on it, You will be given the option to generate the Invoice on related Sale Order.










  • Car Workorders Report: This report will show information about work done on the Car, generated from work order details.


  • Invoice: Create Invoice button will create Invoice for current Sale Order.


  • Access rigths of module : Here you can also assign access rights of following to diffrent users.you can assign following roles to the user go to Settings> Users in form view of user you can found that there are various access rights defined for diffrent users who will interact with the system.
    1> Directeur Commercial
    2> Service Manager
    3> Head Technician
    4> Technician


    • Directeur Commercial : User with this role have all the rights over the car repair order, car dignosis details, workorder details, Quotations made from repair order, and its invoices.
    • Service Manager :User with the rights of service manager have all the rights on repair order, service manager is heavinag limited access on workorder and car diagnosis.
    • Head Technician: User with the rights of head technician have all the rights on car diagnosis order.Head technician is heavinag limited access on repair order.head technician enter details of car dignosis results and assign the work order to the responsible technician.
    • Technician :User with the rights of technician have very limited access he have all the rights related to the work order
